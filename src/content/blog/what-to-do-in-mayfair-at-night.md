## Mayfair After Dark

Mayfair by day is Savile Row tailors and Bond Street shopping. Mayfair by night is a completely different animal. Once the offices close and the shops pull their shutters, the neighbourhood transforms into London's most concentrated nightlife district.

But there's more to Mayfair at night than just nightclubs. Here's the full picture.

## Early Evening: Cocktails

Before the clubs open, Mayfair's cocktail bars set the tone. The best move is to start your evening with drinks before transitioning to a club later.

[Dear Darling](/clubs/dear-darling) is the standout option. Chandeliers, velvet booths, and a cocktail programme that justifies every penny. It opens at 9pm and transitions into late-night energy as the hours pass. You could make it your whole evening or use it as a launchpad.

For something different, explore the hotel bars along Park Lane and Berkeley Square — some of the best cocktail bars in London are hidden inside Mayfair's five-star hotels.

## Dinner That Leads Somewhere

If you want dinner and nightlife in one evening, there are two approaches:

### The Integrated Option

[Maddox](/clubs/maddox) and [Lio Club London](/clubs/lio-london) both offer dinner-to-dance under one roof. At Maddox, you eat Italian upstairs and dance to house music downstairs. At Lio, dinner comes with live performances between courses before the room transforms into a nightclub.

Read our full [dinner and clubbing guide](/blog/dinner-and-clubbing-mayfair) for the details.

### The Two-Stop Option

Have dinner at any of Mayfair's excellent restaurants (the neighbourhood has some of the highest concentration of quality restaurants in the world), then walk to a club afterwards. Everything in Mayfair is within 10-15 minutes on foot.

## The Main Event: Nightclubs

This is what Mayfair is famous for after dark. The clubs here are world-class — exclusive venues, incredible production, and a standard of nightlife that few cities match.

### By Music

- **Hip-hop and RnB:** [Tape London](/clubs/tape-london), [TABU](/clubs/tabu-london), [Libertine](/clubs/libertine), [BEAT London](/clubs/beat-london)
- **House music:** [Maddox](/clubs/maddox), [Cuckoo Club](/clubs/cuckoo-club) (downstairs)
- **Mixed/commercial:** [Luxx Club](/clubs/luxx-club), [Scotch of St James](/clubs/scotch-of-st-james)

### By Experience

- **Entertainment and shows:** [Cirque Le Soir](/clubs/cirque-le-soir), [The London Reign](/clubs/the-london-reign)
- **Exclusive and intimate:** [Tape London](/clubs/tape-london), [Scotch of St James](/clubs/scotch-of-st-james)
- **High energy party:** [Libertine](/clubs/libertine), [BEAT London](/clubs/beat-london)
- **Visual spectacle:** [Luxx Club](/clubs/luxx-club)

Check our [full club rankings](/blog/best-clubs-in-mayfair) or [where to go tonight guide](/where-to-go-tonight) for detailed recommendations.

## The All-Night Option

Most Mayfair clubs close between 3:00 and 3:30am. If you want to go later, [Ministry of Sound](/clubs/ministry-of-sound) in South London runs until 6am on Fridays and Saturdays. It's a 15-minute cab from Mayfair and the experience is worth the trip — multiple rooms, world-class sound, and a crowd that dances until sunrise.

## Planning Your Evening

Here's how a great Mayfair night typically flows:

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 8:00-9:00pm | Dinner (restaurant or Maddox/Lio) |
| 9:00-10:30pm | Cocktails (Dear Darling or hotel bars) |
| 10:30-11:00pm | Arrive at club |
| 11:00pm-3:00am | Dancing, drinks, bottle service |
| 3:00am+ | Late-night food or Ministry of Sound |
